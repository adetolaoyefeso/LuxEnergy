About Us


Lux Energy

An oil servicing company established on the forefront of energy and value creation with focus on; drilling (Onshore and Offshore), rigs, geothermal energy and storage, and general services.
With the recently acquired Arverne Drilling Limited, the companies have a combined experience of over 200 years.

Vision and Ethos

An energy investment organization, we are redefining how to meet the energy needs of Sub-Sahara Africa.

Established in 2020, with primary business operations in Nigeria; Lux Energy is implementing strategy to build a choice asset portfolio of lucrative oil and gas fields at the exploratory and producing stages; via strategic acquisition of equity stakes in Africa-focused E&P companies.

01

Financial Discipline

We ensure that all equity investments are prudently assessed by ascertain the valuation of our select companies and their asset portfolio is reflective of fair market value.

02

Strategic Partnership

As core investors in African exploration and production operations, we select the companies whom we partner with, based on the determined strategic fit of the business operations to our overall vision.

03

Responsibility

As a responsible corporate citizen, Lux Energy will perform due diligence on all company which present an investment opportunity to us.

We ensure that as E&P players, these companies are socially responsible to their host communities and to their society.

Corporate Structure

As a non-operating holding company, Lux Energy represents the interests of its beneficial owners via the acquisition of equity positions in choice E&P companies.

Business Strategy

Our target growth in net reserves and production numbers is based on projected acquisitions in African exploration and production companies, primarily in Nigeria, being our immediate geographic focus.



Strategic Investments

We are careful to invest in companies with a solid management team, who have demonstrated a proven track record for operational excellence and capacity for execution.
Lux Energy also invests in business with good governance practices and boast an existing asset portfolio with significant growth potential and upside.

Business Oversight

We intend to leverage on the experience of the management of Lux Energy, who are successful business practitioners with functional excellence spanning different focal areas of management.

Alliances & Deep Relationships

With over two decades of relationships built across various stakeholder groups in the African region, Lux Energy is able to bring a competitive advantage to the table by navigating the economic and political climates where our business interests are represented.
